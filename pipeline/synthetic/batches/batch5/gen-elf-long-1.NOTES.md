You cannot answer Q3/Q4/Q5 from generic "recycling is good" knowledge — you must have read *this* study's contamination finding and its bounded verdict.

It is how clean."). One **load-bearing quotation** from Feld, deliberately over-absolute ("always, on every batch … no such thing as too much recycled glass") to seed the attribution-swap trap.
