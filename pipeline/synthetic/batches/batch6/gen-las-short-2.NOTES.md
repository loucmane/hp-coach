# NOTES — gen-las-short-2 (slöjdens roll i skolan / craft education)

## Topic & genre rationale
- **Topic:** *slöjd* as a **school subject** and its cognitive role ("thinking with the
  hands") — from my exclusive pool. Deliberately framed as pedagogy/epistemology of the
  subject, **not** woodworking-as-craft, so it does not neighbour the used
  "boat-building/craft timber" or "repair/secondhand economy" items.
- **Genre:** sakprosa / **essä**, reflective first-person, `framing-claim` opening (states
  a common view — slöjd as "vilorum" — to overturn it). Register: reflective essayistic voice
  with a small empirical anchor; sentence length varies (short "Här vill jag genast bromsa mig
  själv." beside longer sentences). Nominalisation (*överföringen*, *monteringsanvisning*).
- **Frame:** title line (not repeated); fictional byline last (Marianne Sjökvist, lärare och
  essäist). No glossary needed.
- **Fiction / anti-plagiarism:** the answerable content is an **invented** classroom follow-up
  (pedagog Ellen Boström, a school in Sandviken). Sandviken is a real town, the study and
  people are fictional. No famous thesis is anchored; a solver must read the invented
  observation to answer Q1.

## Planted trap architecture

### Q1 — `detalj_ospecificerad` (key A)
Planted target (para 2): the **hedged, scoped** observation — pupils who **struggled with
written instructions** nonetheless solved the same **spatial** task quickly at the bench;
transfer to maths was explicitly "svag och osäker" and "gällde långtifrån alla".
- **B = reversed_causality**, hedged in form (no absolutizer): reverses the arrow — bench
  skill later improves reading. Text never claims this. Keeps the key from being the sole
  measured option (M-FORM).
- **C = overgeneralisation** — "alla elever bättre i matematik" absolutises the "svag och
  osäker … långtifrån alla" transfer.
- **D = scope_shift / detail_as_main** — a pedagogical recommendation (remove written
  instructions) the text does not make; the observation concerns what the hand can do.
- All three are plausible-but-wrong readings a passage-blind reader cannot dismiss.

### Q2 — `forfattarens_hallning` (key C)
Whole-text stance: craft is a **form of knowing in its own right** — neither a rest from
thinking nor a servant of other subjects. Spans the opening reversal + para-3 caution +
para-4 thesis.
- **A = detail_as_main** — makes "raise theoretical-subject results" the main task; the
  author explicitly refuses to defend slöjd "enbart som en tjänare åt de teoretiska ämnena".
- **B = reversed_causality** (stance inversion) — the "vilorum/paus" view is exactly the
  opening image the essay overturns ("förhållandet är det omvända").
- **D = overgeneralisation / plausible_worldknowledge** — replacing theoretical subjects for
  struggling readers; a position the text never takes (Boström's transfer was weak).
