2. Evidence — the 2019 pilot delivered on-schedule power "no wind farm could match"; near-modelled output **"in its strongest months"** (the planted hedge Q2/A upgrades).
3. Complication — the **"However"** pivot: the tide was never the hard part; servicing seabed turbines only at slack water, specialised vessels, a failed seal. Costs "swelled into the line that decided whether the whole project made sense."
4. Load-bearing hedged expert quote — Toft: *"the current is bankable ... What we did not show is that you can service the thing cheaply. Those are not the same result."* Economist: "the tide is free, but the boat is not."
5. Verdict — "survives only in part": predictable ≠ affordable.
