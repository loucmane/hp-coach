# gen-las-short-1 — cykelpendlingens infrastruktur (debatt)

## Genre / topic rationale
LÄS short, sakprosa / debatt_opinion. Topic: cycle-commuting infrastructure —
a first-person op-ed arguing that municipalities wrongly measure cycle success
in kilometres, when what actually recruits new cyclists is a *coherent* network
with no dangerous gaps. Opening move = framing-claim; normative modality
(*bör/måste*) and explicit stance throughout, per the debatt preset. Topic is
outside the batch 1–6 used list (no trains/rail, no noise, no urban-quarter
renewal) and is my exclusive pool item.

The passage is deliberately un-tidy (Law 9): it grants the km objection,
concedes land constraints force compromises, and carries concrete residue
(the rondell, the lorries, the two seasons of flow counts) that does not all
point at the thesis.

## Planted trap architecture
Passage plants a hedged, directional, scoped claim: the new lane raised cyclist
counts *only marginally*, and *mostly among already-habituated commuters* — the
hoped-for new cyclists did not appear. Sundqvist's stance ("sammanhanget lockar,
inte sträckan") is the second planted target.

- **Q1 (enligt_texten_detalj), key C.** Paraphrase of the marginal-increase
  finding. Distractors: A = reversed_causality in *hedged form* ("tycktes ha
  dragit sig undan" — keeps a qualifier but flips which group grew, so the key
  is not the sole hedged option); B = overgeneralisation ("öka kraftigt längs
  hela sträckan" vs "bara marginellt"); D = scope_shift to the land-constraint
  paragraph.
- **Q2 (forfattarens_hallning), key B.** The author's explicit thesis. Key is
  the *confident normative* claim (breaks the "qualified answer = correct"
  correlation vs Q1). Distractors: A = the very view he attacks (reversed
  stance / plausible_worldknowledge — the "common sense" a blind reader picks);
  C = detail_as_main (the land concession he dismisses, inflated to his
  position); D = overgeneralisation ("saknar helt betydelse" vs his stated
  "självklart behövs de").

## Self-blind-solve
Solved both from the passage alone, arguing for each non-keyed option:
- Q1 → C only. B contradicts "marginellt"; A reverses the habituated/new
  direction; D belongs to the land paragraph, not what was measured.
- Q2 → B only. A is the attacked view; C is a dismissed concession; D absolutises
  past his explicit hedge.

short-class bands; option-length ratio well under 5.25; key is never the longest
option (Q1 longest = A; Q2 longest = C); keys spread C/B. All entities fictional.
