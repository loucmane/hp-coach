# gen-las-short-2 — svampplockning och allemansrätten (essä)

## Genre / topic rationale
LÄS short, sakprosa / essä (reflective personal essay; opening move = scene).
Topic: mushroom picking and the right of public access (allemansrätten). The
essay's argument is that the right survives not because it is *regulated* but
because most pickers exercise a tacit *måtta* (restraint); large-scale
commercial picking dissolves that unwritten agreement and leaves the right on
weaker ground. Topic is my exclusive pool item and outside the batch 1–6 list.

Deliberately anti-tidy (Law 9): the narrator refuses to scapegoat the commercial
pickers, grants the land took no damage and mushrooms regrow, and explicitly
rejects prohibition signs — genuine ambivalence, not a frictionless parable.
Crucially I avoided anchoring on the famous Ostrom/tragedy-of-the-commons thesis
(Law 1) — the essay never frames this as a commons/free-rider theorem; it stays
on a personal, particular register (mormor's rule, the Torsby vans).

## Planted trap architecture
- **Q1 (enligt_texten_detalj), key D.** Concrete fact: the van pickers' chanterelles
  were to be sold to city restaurants. Key is the *confident specific* claim
  (breaks the qualified-answer correlation vs Q2). Distractors: A = scope_shift
  (the "påse till söndagens omelett" home picker is the contrast figure, not
  these pickers); B = plausible_worldknowledge (invented employer/markägare, no
  textual support); C = overgeneralisation contradicting "Marken tog ingen skada".
- **Q2 (huvudbudskap_syfte, "överensstämmer bäst"), key C.** Whole-text thesis.
  Law 11 respected — no distractor is a verbatim-true detail: A = detail_as_main
  with inverted direction (she explicitly refuses signs); B = overgeneralisation/
  inversion of "aldrig vilat på paragrafer"; D = a true side-detail (mushrooms
  regrow) *distorted* by generalising to "ingen som helst skada av kommersiell
  plockning" and promoted to the message — the essay's unease is the dissolving
  agreement, not ecological damage.
